# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 15, 2026

At **OptionallyBlueStudios**, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect information about you that is necessary to maintain site stability, analyze general user behavior to improve our services, or comply with explicit preferences you select via our Cookie Consent Manager.

### A. Automated Technical Data

When you visit our website, our servers and integrated open-source tools automatically log standard technical data provided by your web browser, including:

* Your device’s Operating System (OS) and version.
* Your browser type, version, and language settings.
* The specific pages or links you access on our site, along with timestamps.
* Technical crash logs and error stack traces.

### B. Analytical and Behavioral Data

If you grant permission, we monitor aggregate usage patterns, such as click metrics, scroll depth, and navigation pathways, to help us design a better user experience.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following specific purposes:

* **To Maintain Operational Integrity:** Tracking and patching software bugs, errors, or application crashes in real time.
* **To Optimize Website Performance:** Analyzing traffic trends to optimize layout, load speeds, and content structure.
* **To Enforce User Choices:** Ensuring that tracking cookies are never initialized unless explicit permission is granted.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

---

## 3. Third-Party Monitoring & Analytics Tools

We carefully manage third-party integrations using an on-site cookie consent system. These tools are split into two distinct tiers: **Essential** (strictly operational) and **Analytics** (user preference-based).

### Tier 1: Essential (Always Active)

* **GlitchTip (Error Tracking):** We use GlitchTip to monitor software bugs and system stability. If an error occurs while you are browsing, GlitchTip captures the error details, browser version, and technical environment layout. Because this tool is strictly operational and does not perform cross-site tracking or user profiling, it is classified as an Essential operational service necessary to maintain our platform.

### Tier 2: Analytics (Consent-Required)

The following tools remain entirely dormant on your machine unless you select "Accept All" or toggle on "Analytics" in our consent settings:

* **Google Analytics 4 (GA4):** Used to measure general engagement metrics like page views, session duration, and approximate geographic location (at an regional/country level). IP addresses are automatically anonymized before transmission to Google servers.
* **Microsoft Clarity:** Used to capture anonymous behavioral visualizations, including heatmaps and scroll recordings, to help us identify broken website elements or confusing interfaces. Sensitive textual form fields are completely masked by default. For details, see the [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement).

---

## 4. Cookies and Tracking Choices

Cookies are small data files placed on your device. We use them to remember your privacy choices and power third-party analytics scripts.

### Managing Preferences

You have absolute control over your cookie profile. You can click the floating **Preferences** icon in the bottom corner of your screen at any time to modify or entirely revoke your consent. Opting out of Analytics will instantly disable and block Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity from executing code or reading cookies on your device.

---

## 5. Data Security & Retention

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information against loss, theft, and unauthorized access.

* Analytical data collected through Google Analytics is retained for a maximum of 14 months before automated purging.
* Operational error logs stored within GlitchTip are systematically deleted after 30 days.

---

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / Global Compliance)

Depending on your geographic location, you may possess specific statutory rights regarding your data, including the right to inspect, correct, or erase the information we hold. Because our configuration automatically anonymizes user profiles and strips identifying metadata upon intake, we generally do not store linkable "Personal Identifiable Information" (PII) on our servers.

Check back here when you want to check if it has been updated. Any changes become effective immediately upon publication to the website.
